Process to be fallowed for a patient in the Hospital

  1. Arrival at Hospital
  2. Registration at Front Desk
    • Provide personal details
    • Provide insurance information (if applicable)
  3. Initial Assessment by Nurse
    • Measurement of vital signs (blood pressure, heart rate, temperature, etc.)
    • Recording of medical history
  4. Consultation with Doctor
    • Discussion of symptoms and concerns
    • Physical examination
    • Prescription or further tests (if necessary)
  5. Diagnostic Tests (if required)
    • Blood tests
    • Imaging (X-rays, MRI, CT scan, etc.)
  6. Treatment Plan
    • Medication
    • Surgery (if required)
    • Physiotherapy or rehabilitation (if required)
  7. Follow-up Instructions
    • Schedule future appointments
    • Instructions for medication and care at home
